# Huy Le

I am a PhD candidate in Statistics at the University of Pittsburgh, with my dissertation defended and degree expected in August 2026. My work focuses on Bayesian modeling, empirical Bayes, high-dimensional inference, spatial dependence, simulation studies, and scalable statistical computation.

I am interested in quantitative research, data science, machine learning, time series, optimization, and applied statistical modeling.

## Selected Work

- Bayesian spatiotemporal modeling for longitudinal high-dimensional data
- Spatial empirical Bayes modeling for heterogeneous correlated data
- Spatial risk forecasting and route optimization
